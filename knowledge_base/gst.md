GST stands for Goods and Services Tax. It is an indirect tax used in India on the supply of goods and services.

For MSMEs, GST-related document management is important because businesses need to track invoices, bills, tax amounts, and filing-related records.

Common GST-related fields in business documents:

* GSTIN
* Invoice number
* Invoice date
* Vendor name
* Customer name
* Taxable amount
* CGST
* SGST
* IGST
* Total tax
* Total invoice amount

Common GST document problems faced by MSMEs:

* Missing GSTIN in invoices
* Incorrect tax calculation
* Lost purchase bills
* Poor record keeping
* Difficulty matching sales and purchase invoices
* Manual GST data preparation
* Confusion between taxable amount and total amount

* Extracting GST-related fields from invoices
* Checking whether tax fields are missing
* Summarizing total tax from documents
* Organizing invoices and purchase bills
* Helping the owner understand GST-related document issues in simple language
