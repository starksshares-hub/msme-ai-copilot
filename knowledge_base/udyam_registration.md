The Udyam Registration MechanismTo access the benefits of the revised classification, enterprises must complete the registration process on the official government Udyam Registration Portal. This registration is fully paperless, digital, and free of charge.The registration process involves the following sequential steps:The entrepreneur inputs their Aadhaar number on the homepage, which triggers an automated OTP-based validation process.Upon validation, the portal redirects the user to a PAN verification page to retrieve organizational details directly from the CBDT database.The registrant inputs the enterprise's investment in plant, machinery, or equipment (excluding land and buildings) along with its annual turnover details.Following the submission of the final OTP, the system generates an official Udyam e-registration certificate containing a dynamic QR code. This QR code serves as a single, verified reference for regulatory compliance, banking assessments, and access to government welfare schemes.
