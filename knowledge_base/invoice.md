Invoice management is important for MSMEs because invoices show how much money customers owe to the business.

An invoice usually contains:

* Invoice number
* Customer name
* Vendor or business name
* Invoice date
* Due date
* Line items
* Quantity
* Unit price
* Tax
* Total amount
* Payment status

Common invoice problems faced by MSMEs:

* Customers delay payments
* Invoice numbers are not tracked properly
* Duplicate invoices may be created
* Payment due dates are missed
* Business owners do not know how much money is still unpaid
* Manual invoice tracking takes too much time

An AI copilot can help by:

* Finding overdue invoices
* Summarizing unpaid amounts
* Explaining which customers are delaying payments
* Detecting duplicate invoice numbers
* Extracting invoice data from PDFs or text files
* Creating monthly invoice summaries

Example questions:

* Which invoices are overdue?
* How much money is unpaid?
* Which customer has the highest pending amount?
* Are there duplicate invoices?
* What is the total invoice value this month?
